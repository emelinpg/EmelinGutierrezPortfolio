Hello!!

Emelin Gutierrez

Web Developer/Graphic Designer

I am a web developer and graphic designer who enjoys making simple, clean designs that are functional and easy to use. I like to add my own personal touch to the majority of my work. I am inspired by unique and fun designs that maintain that a classic feel while catching users attention. I am into old school horror movies, and the 80s, which influences me to add some retro elements into my design to give a nostalgic feeling while still very modern. When I am not on my computer, I enjoy photography, hedgehogs and pizza.
